Holiday Roasted Vegetables

Cook Time: 25 Minutes

Yield: 4 Servings

Description:

To break up the richness, I always love adding these simple, no-fuss roasted vegetables to the menu. Brussels sprouts and carrots are the stars of the show here, while dried cranberries and balsamic vinegar add some sweetness and tartness

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 2 large carrots, peeled, sliced into 1/2” pieces
  • 2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp. balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tsp. chopped rosemary leaves
  • 2 tsp. chopped thyme leaves
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 c. toasted pecans
  • 1/2 c. dried cranberries

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°. Scatter vegetables on a large baking sheet. Toss with oil, balsamic vinegar, rosemary, and thyme. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Bake for 40 - 60 minutes, until the vegetable are tender, shaking the pan halfway through.
  3. Before serving, toss roasted vegetables with pecans and cranberries.
