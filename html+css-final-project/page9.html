The End of An Era

What led to the end of the band?

Brockhampton's end stemmed from a mix of internal tensions, creative burnout, managing a large group dynamic, the fallout from sexual misconduct allegations against Ameer Vann (leading to his departure), a rocky major label deal (RCA Records), and the desire for members to pursue solo careers, culminating in their "breakup" albums The Family and TM. They essentially ran their natural course as a collective, deciding to part ways after a decade.
